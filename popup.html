Distraction Reroute instantly redirects distracting domains—or wildcard/path matches—to more productive alternatives. Toggle preset categories or add custom rules; every change auto-saves and syncs via Chrome Sync.

  • Uses Chrome Declarative Net Request for private, fast redirects
  • Wildcard & path support (e.g. *://*.site.com/path/*)
  • All settings stored locally & synced across devices
